What is the fuel cost calculator?
The fuel cost calculator is a tool that calculates the amount of fuel you will need for a trip. It also calculates the amount of money you will spend to buy fuel.
How to calculate fuel cost?
To calculate fuel cost you should use the following formula:
trip cost = (distance / fuel efficiency) * fuel price
Why use fuel cost calculator?
The fuel cost calculator is used in order to measure the cost needed to buy fuel.
